FIPS 140-2 Certified Flash Storage

T5PF and T5PFLC 2.5" SSDs and M.2 2280 Drives

The T5PF and T5PFLC Flash storage drives are fully FIPS 140-2 compliant. The advantage of both compared to other Flash storage is the chip in T5PF/T5PFLC drives are certified so if there’s a technology change to the controller or NAND the SSDs do not need to be recertified - the FIPS boundary is limited to the chip.

The T5PF is TLC NAND and comes in capacities from 480GB to 4TB of storage in both industrial and commercial temps. The T5PFLC is also TLC NAND and has capacities of 120GB to 2TB for the 2.5” and capacities of 240GB to 1TB for the M.2 2280 form factor. Again, all in commercial and industrial temps.

Features & Benifits:

  • AES-XTS 256-bit encryption automatically protects all data written to the drive
  • Passes MIL-STD-810G
  • True industrial grade storage solution for "no-compromise" application
  • Advanced flash management for enhanced reliability and durabilty
  • Self-monitoring Analysis and Reporting Technology (S.M.A.R.T.) support
  • Hardware erase triggers and hardware write protection
  • FIPS 140-2 compliant
